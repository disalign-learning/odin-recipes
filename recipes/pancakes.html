Pancakes

I think that this entry speaks for itself. Pancakes are a staple in MANY peoples lives and I believe I found the best way to make them. Check it out!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose | plain flour, (290 g | 10 oz)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar or sweetener, (60g | 2 oz)
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups milk, (440ml)
  • 1/4 cup butter, (60g | 2 oz)
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
